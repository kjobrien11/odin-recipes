Burger

Description:

Mama's home made pizza! The best pizza in the world. Easy five ingredient, five step recipe that anyone can make.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 slice American cheese
  • 1 bun
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 piece of lettuce

Steps:

  1. Cook burger as desired
  2. Place cheese on top of burger
  3. Place burger onto bun
  4. Add toppings as desired
  5. Enjoy!
