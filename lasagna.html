Lasagna

Description:

Mama's home made lasagna! The best lasagna in the world. Easy five ingredient, five step recipe that anyone can make.

Ingredients:

  • 10oz Lasagna Noodles
  • 1.5 lbs beef
  • 3 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven 375 degrees
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil
  3. Brown ground beef in large skillet
  4. Layer noodles ground beef and tomato sauce in pan
  5. Cook for 20 minutes and enjoy!
