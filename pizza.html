Pizza

Description:

Mama's home made pizza! The best pizza in the world. Easy five ingredient, five step recipe that anyone can make.

Ingredients:

  • jar of tomato sauce
  • 1lb mozarella cheese
  • 1 stick pepperroni
  • 2lb dough
  • Oven

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees
  2. Roll out dough into circle
  3. Add pepperroni and cheese
  4. Cook in oven for 15 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
